Dominican Software Engineer | Entrepreneur | Author | Coach

Empowering The Next Billion Entrepreneurs

I am a seasoned software engineer and a proud alumnus of Singularity University (GSP2012). With a rich history as an Entrepreneur in Residence (Mountain View - 2018 / Denmark 2019), and an OpenExO Certified Advisor, Trainer, and Coach, I thrive at the intersection of technology, entrepreneurship, and innovation.

As the Head Coach of Doulab, I drive initiatives to massively accelerate entrepreneurship, aiding aspiring entrepreneurs in building sustainable, exponentially accelerated organizations for meaningful change. I am also the author of "The MicroCanvas," an open-source framework aimed at transforming organizational structures for the better.

With two decades of consulting experience in both public and private sectors, I have significantly contributed to open data initiatives, interoperability, and business model innovation across various international platforms. My collaborative efforts with the Dominican Republic Government on IT standards and Cybersecurity readiness for the Financial Sector have been notably impactful.

Above all, my journey is enriched by a personal relationship with Jesus, a loving marriage, the joy of fathering three, and a relentless passion for leveraging technology to positively impact lives.
